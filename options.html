Need help setting it up?

For Dropbox:

  1. Choose Dropbox as the storage provider
  2. Click Connect Dropbox
  3. Approve the Dropbox sign-in window

For Google Drive:

  1. Choose Google Drive as the storage provider
  2. Click Connect Google Drive
  3. Approve the Google sign-in window

Want to use your own OAuth?

For Dropbox:

  1. Open Dropbox Create app
  2. Choose Scoped access, then Full Dropbox
  3. Turn on files.content.write
  4. Open your Dropbox app Settings and add the exact Redirect URI shown below
  5. Copy the App key and paste it under Advanced setup here
  6. Press the Connect Dropbox button

For Google Drive:

  1. Open Google Cloud OAuth clients
  2. Create a Web application OAuth client
  3. Add the exact Redirect URI shown on this page as an authorized redirect URI
  4. Copy the Client ID, paste it into Google OAuth web client ID under Advanced setup, then save
  5. Click Connect Google Drive and approve the Google sign-in window

If some X media will not download and upload:

  1. Sign in on x.com or twitter.com in this browser
  2. Open Advanced setup
  3. Click Connect my X account, then try the post again

If an Instagram post or reel says it needs your account:

  1. Sign in on instagram.com in this browser
  2. Open Advanced setup
  3. Click Connect my Instagram account, then try the post again
